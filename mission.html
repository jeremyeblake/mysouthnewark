Our Mission

Mission

The mission of the South Newark Civic Association is to build harmony amongst neighbors through the development of service learning activities and the promotion of volunteering.

Purpose

The purpose of the South Newark Civic Association is to increase fellowship and collective awareness of issues in the southern neighborhoods of the city of Newark.

We believe that

  • All individuals are unique and worthy of respect
  • All individuals are responsible for their behavior
  • Given the opportunity, everyone can reach her/his full potential
  • The family is a primary influence in a personâ€™s life
  • Change is constant; by embracing change, we provide growth opportunity
  • Diversity is a positive aspect in our community
  • The investment we make in volunteering determines our future
  • Our residents must work together to assure that our neighborhood is preserved

Our goals are

  • To engage 100 percent of our residents
  • To welcome all residents to participate in our efforts
  • To achieve a collective awareness of issues challenging South Newark
  • To actively advocate for positive change
  • To build harmony amongst neighbors

We will always

  • Make the neighborhood our number one priority
  • Apply current research to accomplish our mission
  • Recognize the diverse needs of each resident
  • Involve all segments of our community in strategic planning
  • Consider community and organization relationships in decision making

We will never

  • Implement new initiatives without considering the total impact
  • Implement new initiatives which are not consistent with our mission and purpose
  • Implement new initiatives without appropriate community development
  • Compromise our mission

Our Strategies

1. We will develop and implement a plan to assure all residents are engaged.

  1. 1.1 We will regularly gather resident input to assure that we are addressing neighborhood needs.
  2. 1.2 We will be patient and take small steps to reach out to neighbors on a one-on-one basis to improve trust, engagement and fellowship.
  3. 1.3 We will work to assure that all people feel welcome to our lunches, meetings and events.
  4. 1.4 We will reach out to the business owners, churches and other organizations in our neighborhood.

2. We will develop and implement ways to improve the perception of South Newark.

  1. 2.1 We will work to change the negative stereotypes associated with South Newark.
  2. 2.2 We will take on efforts to improve neighborhood pride.

3. We will align resources and create partnerships to ensure the success of our neighborhood.

  1. 3.1 We will collaborate with property owners, tenants and appropriate organizations to improve the appearance of our neighborhood.
  2. 3.2 We will collaborate with appropriate individuals and organizations to advocate for fixed route busing.
  3. 3.3 We will collaborate with appropriate organizations to concentrate on efforts to reduce poverty.
  4. 3.4 We will collaborate with appropriate organizations to support parent mentoring and decrease juvenile crime.
  5. 3.5 We will collaborate with appropriate organizations to support individuals with substance abuse issues.
  6. 3.6 We will collaborate with appropriate organizations to improve the education of our neighborhood.

4. We will develop and implement organization that will ensure we educate ourselves on issues.

  1. 4.1 We will learn how to organize ourselves so that we are speaking as a collective voice.
  2. 4.2 We will learn about the problems and solutions related to dealing with vacant homes and properties.
  3. 4.3 We will learn about the problems and solutions related to providing livable affordable
  4. housing.
  5. 4.4 We will learn about ways to promote home ownership in our neighborhood.
  6. 4.5 We will learn about community policing as a means to improve our neighborhood.
  7. 4.6 We will learn about the matters related to community development including family dining options for our neighborhood.
  8. 4.7 We will learn about the various recreation options available to our residents.

5. We will identify ways to unite the citizens of South Newark for unified representation.
